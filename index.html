Biography

I received the Ph.D. degree in Geotechnical Engineering from Hohai University, Nanjing, Jiangsu Province, China in 2024, supervised by Professor Jian Ji and the double Ph.D. degree in Geotechnical Engineering from Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya(UPC), Barcelona, Spain in 2024, supervised by Professor Hürlimann Marcel. My current research interest includes landslides, geological hazard.
